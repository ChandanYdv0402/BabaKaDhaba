Our Story

Every Fla vor Tells a Story

Baba Ka Dhaba’s rise to fame was driven by a whirlwind of controversy and media attention. The dhaba's unconventional cooking methods were an underlying factor that enhanced the flavor in a way that no one discussed. The turning point came when Baba accused his fundraiser of embezzling money, sparking a dramatic downfall. Despite this controversy, Baba's food, created a taste that spoiled many palates, leaving them hooked on Baba’s distinct and aged flavors while also saving his grace. Despite the unhygienic food, the dhaba's rise is a testament to how a captivating narrative can turn controversy into success.

Why Choose Us

Our Strength

  • Hygienic Food

    We challenge conventional methods. Rather than using gloves, which we find unhygienic due to microplastic deposits, we prefer handling everything barehanded. We don’t bother extracting spices from plants; instead, we utilize the natural accumulation of spices on our bodies. Our version of hygiene prioritizes flavor and sustainability.

  • Fresh Environment

    We take pride in our unconventional approach to freshness. Our uncleaned, smoke-blackened rooftops preserve our unique flavors and help new chefs identify our signature taste by aroma alone. The intentional lack of ventilation captures the essence of our food, mingling with the cooks and enhancing flavors through increased sweating and spice reminders.

  • Skilled Chefs

    Our chefs, guided by the supreme Baba, master exceptional skills and unique techniques. Each chef's distinct sweat—accumulated through their individual cooking styles—imparts a personal touch to every dish. This sweat-based distinction ensures that each meal carries a unique signature and ensures that every meal reflects the supreme guidance of Baba.

  • Event & Party

    At Baba Ka Dhaba, we transform every event and party into an extraordinary affair. Under the supreme guidance of Baba, we offer a distinctive celebration with our unconventional dishes. Our approach ensures that each event is not only memorable but also sets a new standard. Expect a touch of the unexpected that makes every occasion truly special.
